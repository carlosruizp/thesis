Eduardo C. Garrido-Merchán received his B.S. degree in Computer Science (2013) from Universidad Pontificia de Comillas being the best student of the promotion and M.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence (2015) from Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. He is currently studying his Ph.D. degree about Bayesian Optimization and Gaussian Processes from Universidad Autónoma de Madrid. He is also a Teaching Assistant at the department of Software Engineering in Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, Spain. He has co-authored 15 international journal and conference papers in the fields of Machine Learning, Bayesian Optimization, Graphical Models and Natural Language Processing. His current interests deal with Bayesian Optimization, Gaussian Processes, Bayesian Statistics and several applications of these methodologies.
